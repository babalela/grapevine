About


Grapevine is a Zambian online advertising platform.

Advertisers

We welcome advertisers who wish to reach our creative and influential Zambian audience.

Members

Grapevine membership is by invitation. However, get in touch and we may be able to accommodate you.

  • Pencil Case Studios

Details

Seven advertisement slots are available every week. Each ad is published across all websites on our network. For exclusivity, only one advertisement (yours!) will be visible on a page at any one time.

Format

Each ad contains a 160×90 pixel graphic and allows for up to 90 characters of text.

Pricing

The current rate to advertise on Grapevine is K50,000 in Zain Talk-time per advertisement per week.
