About

Grapevine is an online advertising network

Getting you a front row seat on the conversations of influential and opinionated people, Grapevine is like a fly on the wall — without the creepiness.

We hand-select our advertisers and products based on what is relevant to our audience.

Your advertisement will be the only one on the page of the website being viewed. No need to worry about competing for attention.

Sell our audience something truly great and they will reward you.

Membership

Joining as a Grapevine publisher is only possible by invitation.

Format and Pricing

The seven advertisement slots each month are rotated across all sites on our network.

A single advertisement allows for a 160 × 90 pixel graphic and up to 90 characters of text within which to express oneself.

The current rate to advertise on Grapevine is USD$216 per advertisement per month.
